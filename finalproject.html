Welcome to my Final Project!

The program my partner and I made, named Rocket Man or “RocketMan”, is
a game that is very similar to a famous game from a couple of years ago. Rocket
Man is a game akin to the hit mobile game “Flappy Bird”, where they both share
the same basic idea that you play as a bird who avoids pipes/obstacles. Though,
this game has its fair share of differences. In the game my partner Andrew King
and I made, we have added other things, such as levels, gems (or coins), and different control styles.
Something that my partner and I did to have players
differentiate the two games from each other is not only add gems and levels, but
we also added a game mechanism that makes the game more difficult as the play
progresses through each level. As the player avoids the obstacles, they earn a
point to their score and also earn an extra point to their score whenever they get a
gem, and we hope to have every player achieve the highest possible score possible!
